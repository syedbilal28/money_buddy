How it Works

Ayutogroup enables small groups of individuals to come together and save money for short term goals. Here's how it works: members (could be friends or strangers) get together online and decide to contribute a fixed amount of money into a savings pot each month. Every month one member of the group gets to take home the entire savings pot. This continues until everyone in the group has had a chance to take the pot.
